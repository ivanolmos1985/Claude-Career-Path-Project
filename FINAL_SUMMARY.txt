SUBDOMAIN ISSUE RESOLUTION
================================================================================

Question: Why couldn't we change the subdomain to "careerpath.pages.dev"?

Answer:
The subdomain is based on the PROJECT NAME in Cloudflare Pages.
Your project is named "claude-career-path-project" in Cloudflare,
which automatically generates the subdomain "claude-career-path-project.pages.dev"

To change this would require:
1. Renaming the project in Cloudflare (extremely difficult, not recommended)
2. OR registering a custom domain (e.g., careerpath.com)

Detailed explanation with solutions available in:
‚Üí CLOUDFLARE_SUBDOMAIN_FIX.md

Current URL is suitable for:
- Development and testing
- Internal team use
- MVP demonstrations
- Proof of concept

For production use with branded domain, register a custom domain
and connect it to Cloudflare Pages (see CLOUDFLARE_SUBDOMAIN_FIX.md)
